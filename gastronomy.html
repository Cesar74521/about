Way of eating the typical dishes / food

The Spain gastronomy is world famous and some of its typical dishes are so popular that you have to try them during your trip to Spain. All the recipes that we propose below are so well known that it will be easy for you to find them when ordering in a restaurant. And then, to upload photos to the internet to make your friends envious. Remember that these are just a few examples.

Coco from Madrid: A very warm stew is a pleasure available to everyone. Although there are several methods of cooking, the one known as Madrid has a traditional way of eating it: start with a tasty soup with noodles, then chickpeas and vegetables and finish with meats and sausages.
